# SMR Equity Research Pitch – NuScale Power

Equity research and stock pitch on NuScale Power (SMR) prepared for the 360 Huntington Fund at Northeastern University, including financial analysis and investment recommendation.

## Overview

Completed as a **Junior Equity Analyst** for the **360 Huntington Fund** at Northeastern University. Conducted fundamental analysis on NuScale Power (NYSE: SMR), a small modular nuclear reactor company, and presented a buy/sell recommendation to the fund's investment committee.
